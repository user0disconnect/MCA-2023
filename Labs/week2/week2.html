Week 2: Notation Basics

Task: Editing Notes After OMR Manipulation

For this week, I decided to transcribe Arvo Pärt's composition Spiegel im Spiegel using MuseScore, the finished file can be found below or here.

Here are a few changes I made to the file, as the program was not transcribing the notes correctly at first.

  1. I changed the arrangement because, at the beginning, the OMR engine counted everything as Voice.
  2. Changed the tempo from 120 to the original 84.
  3. Changed the time signature from 3/4 to 6/4.
  4. The engine didn't recognize the octaves, so I added an ottava 8va alta.
  5. Also, I added the name of the piece and the artist.
